Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity.

My culture has helped shape my personal values by experiencing life within a whanau, and a greater community.

Ive always feel better working together with people, and have always made an effort to reach out. A key personal value to me is Whakawhanaungatanga - or working together for the good of all.

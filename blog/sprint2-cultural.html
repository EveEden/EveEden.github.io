My Learning Agreement

My plan forward

I plan to manage my workload by scheduling my time by week - this includes my family time, training schedule, studytime and work. I will overview my workload, add it to my schedule with the timebox guidelines and assess it daily. My aim is to contribute to the slack community weekly, even if its just giving props to other peopls work, as well as checking in with my co-ordinator atleast every 2 weeks.

She has been my biggest motivation to making these changes adn commiting to this course of study - I know she will be great motivation. Facillitation Expectations

I hope to be able to reach out to my facillitation team with questions. My experience so far has been positive, and Ive found a huge level of understanding and support from this team, right from the first time I said "I want to take this course".

Part 2

Im optimistic, confident and unafraid of a challenge. These things can also be my limitations as I often bite off more than I can chew and lose motivatiton. At times this has put me off setting ambitious goals and ive settled for a career that is easier to achive in the short term but not fulfilling.
